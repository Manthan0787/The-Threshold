About The Competition

A new era of competitive challenges

THE THRESHOLD is an innovative survival competition platform designed for students who dare to push beyond their limits. Through four unique environmental challenges, competitors test their problem-solving skills, teamwork, and adaptability under pressure.

Each challenge simulates extreme conditions where participants must think strategically, collaborate effectively, and make split-second decisions. This isn't just about physical endurance—it's about mental resilience, technical skill, and the will to overcome.

CRYOGENIC ESCAPE

High Team 3-4 60 min

Locked in a chamber dropping to -40°C. Solve code puzzles, redirect heat sources, and escape before the temperature kills you.

PRESSURE DROP

Extreme Team 2-4 45 min
